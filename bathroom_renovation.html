Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about bathroom remodeling.

How long does a typical bathroom remodel take?
A standard bathroom remodel usually takes between 3 to 5 weeks. This timeline can vary depending on the complexity of the project, such as moving plumbing lines or waiting for custom materials. We provide a detailed schedule before starting so you know what to expect.
Do I need a permit for my bathroom renovation?
In most cases in Irvine, yes. If we are changing the footprint, electrical, or plumbing, a permit is required. As a licensed general contractor, we handle all the permitting processes for you, ensuring your project is up to code and fully legal.
Can I stay in my home during the renovation?
Absolutely. We work to contain dust and debris to the work area using plastic sheeting and floor protection. However, the bathroom being worked on will be out of commission, so you'll need another functional bathroom available during the process.
