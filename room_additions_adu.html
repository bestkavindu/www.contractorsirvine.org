Types of Accessory Dwelling Units

Customized solutions to fit your property and lifestyle needs.

Detached ADU

A standalone structure separate from the main house. Perfect for maximum privacy, creating a backyard cottage, guest house, or rental unit.

Attached ADU

An addition connected directly to the primary residence. Ideal for expanding living space, adding a master suite, or creating an in-law unit with shared walls.

Garage Conversion

Transforming an existing garage into a functional living space. A cost-effective way to add an ADU without altering the building footprint.
