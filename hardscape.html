Our Design & Build Process

Turning your outdoor vision into a durable reality.

1

Design & Excavation

Every great project starts with a solid foundation. We create a detailed layout based on your vision, then excavate to the proper depth. We address drainage and soil stability to ensure your new hardscape won't settle or shift.

2

Base Prep & Installation

We install a heavy-duty compacted gravel base, followed by a precise layer of bedding sand. Whether placing interlocking pavers, pouring concrete, or stacking retaining walls, our craftsmanship focuses on level surfaces and tight joints.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your hardscaping questions.

Pavers vs. Concrete: Which is better?
Pavers are generally superior for durability because they are flexible and won't crack like concrete slabs. If a paver gets stained or damaged, it can be easily replaced. However, stamped concrete can be a more budget-friendly option for large areas.
Do I need a permit for a patio?
In Irvine, small low-level patios often don't need a permit, but anything with utilities (gas/electric), covers, or retaining walls over 3 feet usually does. We handle all the permitting and checking with the city for you.
How do I maintain my pavers?
We recommend sweeping regularly and hosing them down. Every 2-3 years, it's a good idea to replenish joint sand and reseal the surface to keep the colors vibrant and prevent weed growth.
